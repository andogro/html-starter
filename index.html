Strategic Focus Areas

In 2024 and the first half of 2025 we were focused on growing our user base and clientele. We are now the #1 block explorer in terms of EVM reach, and are shifting focus to reduce costs and increase recurring revenue.

Reduce Infrastructure Costs

  • Cloud infrastructure optimization across multiple providers
  • Data center location exploration to reduce bare metal costs
  • Optimizing cloud/bare-metal balance for cost efficiency
  • Database optimizations targeting 30% reduction in storage needs

Increase Revenue Generation

  • Premium API launch with immediate $100K/month revenue based on current usage. We will launch this service in 1-2 months.
  • Institutional data packages focusing on compliance & stablecoin analytics. In development.
  • Full-featured wallet integration. In active exploration.
  • Advanced data analytics services for supported chains. In research.

Maintain and Expand Pipeline

  • Our client base continues to grow month over month with very little attrition
  • We have signed agreements with several large new clients and will begin dev shortly (not announced yet or under NDA)
  • We are in discussions with additional RaaS providers and ecosystems to bring on more L2s and L3s

Our new widgets feature delivers valuable third-party insights about addresses and contracts. Widget partners create valuable backlinks to Blockscout, driving both user engagement and referral traffic.

Our MCP connects LLMs to Blockscout data. It is available for public use through our own API, Alibaba Higress and Smithery AI Marketplaces and being used by projects like SettleMint Networks.
